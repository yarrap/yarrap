

### About

Hey, there! I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. I am passionate about Artificial Intelligence, particularly in Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Responsible AI, and Information Retrieval.

I bring approximately 3.5 years of experience in this domain, including 2 years of industry experience as a Senior Data Scientist, where I primarily worked in e-commerce domains and applied machine learning to real-world problems. In parallel, I have strong research experience in NLP from my work at UMass, where I studied out-of-distribution (OOD) settings in large language models and improved mathematical abilities of an LLM. Previously, at RAPP, I worked on time-series forecasting and topic modeling, and at LatentView Analytics, I contributed to integrating HubSpot with Google Gemini.

Currently, I'm actively seeking full-time roles starting immediately across the United States that align with my interests and career goals. I would be delighted to connect and explore potential opportunities together!
